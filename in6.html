Cartosat – Advancing Earth Observation

Cartosat satellites are a series of high-resolution Earth observation satellites used primarily for cartographic applications. They have been instrumental in rural and urban planning, road network monitoring, and border surveillance.

With capabilities reaching sub-meter spatial resolution, Cartosat satellites provide stereoscopic imagery and are integral to India’s defense and civilian imaging requirements. The series continues to evolve with Cartosat-3 having advanced optical sensors and agility.

Specifications:

Orbit:Sun-synchronous Orbit
Resolution:0.25–1 m (Cartosat-3)
Use:Mapping, Urban Planning
