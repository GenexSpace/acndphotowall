SPADEX – DOCKING IN SPACE

SPADEX (Space Docking Experiment) is an upcoming mission by ISRO to demonstrate autonomous docking between two spacecraft in orbit. This capability is essential for future space station operations, satellite servicing, and long-duration missions. The mission involves two small satellites that will practice formation flying and docking maneuvers in orbit.
