CARTOSAT – ADVANCING EARTH OBSERVATION

The Cartosat series is a family of Indian Earth observation satellites used for high-resolution cartographic and surveillance applications. They have aided in border monitoring, agricultural planning, and disaster response. Orbital Details:
- Orbit: Sun-synchronous polar orbit
- Altitude: ~630–650 km
- Inclination: ~97.5°
Technical Specifications:
- Resolution: Up to 0.25 m (Cartosat-3)
- Mass: ~1,600 kg (Cartosat-3)
- Payload: Panchromatic and Multispectral cameras
