ISRO’S RECORD-BREAKING LAUNCH – 104 SATELLITES (2017)

On February 15, 2017, ISRO set a world record by launching 104 satellites in one mission aboard PSLV-C37.

This feat demonstrated India’s capabilities in rideshare and microsatellite deployment.

Launch Facts:


- Vehicle: PSLV-C37


- Record: 104 satellites in a single launch


- Clients: International customers including Planet Labs


- Launch Site: Satish Dhawan Space Centre, Sriharikota


Technical Highlights:

  • Launch Vehicle: PSLV-C37
  • Satellites Deployed: 104
  • Main Payload: Cartosat-2D
  • Orbit: Sun-synchronous, ~505 km altitude
