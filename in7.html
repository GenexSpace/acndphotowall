RLV-TD – India’s Space Shuttle Prototype (2016)

The Reusable Launch Vehicle–Technology Demonstrator (RLV-TD) was India’s first step toward developing a space shuttle-like vehicle. The experimental flight, conducted on May 23, 2016, tested hypersonic flight, autonomous landing, and controlled reentry.

RLV-TD successfully completed its suborbital flight and landed in the Bay of Bengal as planned. The mission validated key technologies and brought India closer to realizing low-cost, reusable launch systems for future space missions.

Specifications:

Launch Date:May 23, 2016
Flight Duration:770 seconds
Landing:Bay of Bengal
