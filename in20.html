Nigar Shaji – Mission Director of Aditya-L1

Nigar Shaji, the mission director of Aditya-L1, has been instrumental in India’s solar observatory program. With over 30 years of experience at ISRO, she has led critical satellite and payload systems engineering projects.

Her leadership reflects the growing contribution of Indian women scientists in space exploration and mission management.
