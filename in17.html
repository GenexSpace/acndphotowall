PSLV – INDIA’S WORKHORSE ROCKET

The Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) is one of India’s most successful rockets, known for its reliability and versatility.

It is capable of launching satellites into sun-synchronous and polar orbits and has also launched missions to the Moon and Mars.

Performance:


- Payload to SSO: ~1,750 kg


- Notable Missions: Chandrayaan-1, Mangalyaan


- Record: 104 satellites launched in a single mission


- Launches: 50+ successful launches


Technical Specifications:

  • Stages: 4 (Solid + Liquid + Solid + Liquid)
  • Height: 44.4 m
  • Liftoff Mass: ~320,000 kg
  • Payload: 1,750 kg to Sun-synchronous orbit
  • Versions: PSLV-G, PSLV-XL, PSLV-CA
