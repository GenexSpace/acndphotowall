ASTROSAT – INDIA’S SPACE OBSERVATORY (2015)

ASTROSAT, launched in 2015, is India's first dedicated space observatory. It observes the universe in ultraviolet, visible, and X-ray bands. The satellite has helped study stars, galaxies, black holes, and cosmic radiation, and has contributed data to many international studies in astrophysics. It reflects India’s growing capability in space science.
