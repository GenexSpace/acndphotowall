SpaDeX – Docking in Space (Upcoming)

SpaDeX, or Space Docking Experiment, is a forthcoming ISRO mission to demonstrate autonomous docking capability in orbit. It involves launching two spacecraft that will perform controlled maneuvers and dock with each other in space.

The success of SpaDeX will enable future applications such as satellite refueling, orbital servicing, and modular space station development, reinforcing India's position in advanced orbital technologies.

Specifications:

Mission Type:Technology Demonstration
Status:Planned
Capabilities:Docking, Formation Flying
