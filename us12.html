Dragon & Crew Dragon – Commercial Space Travel (2012–Present)

Dragon is a class of spacecraft developed by SpaceX to transport cargo and crew to and from the International Space Station. The original Cargo Dragon first flew in 2010, followed by Crew Dragon (also known as Dragon 2), which carried astronauts to orbit beginning in 2020.

Crew Dragon became the first commercial spacecraft to deliver astronauts to the ISS under NASA’s Commercial Crew Program. With autonomous docking, emergency abort systems, and reusable design, Dragon represents a new era in human spaceflight.
