104 Satellites in One Launch – World Record (2017)

On February 15, 2017, ISRO set a world record by launching 104 satellites aboard PSLV-C37 in a single mission. The primary satellite was Cartosat-2D, accompanied by 103 co-passenger nanosatellites.

The mission demonstrated India's prowess in rideshare launches and showcased its capabilities in managing complex satellite deployments.

Specifications:

Launch Date:February 15, 2017
Rocket:PSLV-C37
Satellites:104 in single launch
