CHANDRAYAAN-2 – INDIA’S SECOND MOON MISSION

Chandrayaan-2 attempted a soft landing on the Moon with an orbiter, lander and rover. The orbiter remains operational.

Mission Info:

  • Launch Date: July 22, 2019
  • Orbiter: Active
  • Lander: Vikram (unsuccessful)
