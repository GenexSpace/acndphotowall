CHANDRAYAAN-3 – SOFT LANDING NEAR THE MOON’S SOUTH POLE (2023)

Chandrayaan-3 was launched on July 14, 2023, and successfully soft-landed on the Moon’s south polar region on August 23, 2023, making India the first country to do so. It included a lander (Vikram) and rover (Pragyan), which conducted experiments on lunar soil and surface composition. Orbital Details:
- Final Lunar Orbit: ~100 km circular
- Lander Descent: Controlled soft-landing from 30 km
Technical Specifications:
- Lander Mass: ~1,700 kg
- Rover Mass: ~26 kg
- Power: Solar panels
- Instruments: RAMBHA, ILSA, LASER Retroreflector, APXS, and LIBS
