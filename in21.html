SSLV – India’s Small Satellite Launcher (2022)

The Small Satellite Launch Vehicle (SSLV) is ISRO’s latest addition to India’s family of launch vehicles. Designed for rapid, on-demand launches, SSLV is optimized for deploying small satellites weighing up to 500 kg to Low Earth Orbit (LEO).

SSLV had its successful maiden flight on February 10, 2023, delivering EOS-07 and two passenger payloads to orbit. It is intended to operate with low turnaround time and minimal infrastructure, providing India with a cost-effective platform to compete in the booming small satellite market.

Specifications:

First Successful Launch:February 10, 2023
Payload Capacity:500 kg to LEO
Stages:3 solid + 1 velocity trimming module
Launch Site:SDSC, Sriharikota
