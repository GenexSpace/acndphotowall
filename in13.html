India’s Thriving Private Space Sector

India’s private space ecosystem has seen a surge since 2020, following policy reforms and increased government support for startups and academia. Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s interaction with private space stakeholders in December 2020 symbolized this shift, ushering in an era of collaborative innovation and commercialization.

Private players like Skyroot Aerospace, Agnikul Cosmos, and Pixxel are now developing launch vehicles, propulsion systems, and EO satellites. Enabled by IN-SPACe and collaborations with ISRO, this sector is poised to transform India into a global hub for affordable and scalable space technologies.

Specifications:

Milestone:Policy Reform & IN-SPACe Establishment (2020)
PM Event:Interaction with startups on Dec 14, 2020
Private Players:Skyroot Aerospace, Agnikul Cosmos, Pixxel, Bellatrix
Enablers:IN-SPACe, NSIL, Technology Transfer from ISRO
Focus Areas:Launch vehicles, satellite manufacturing, data services
