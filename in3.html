APPLE – EXPERIMENTAL COMMUNICATION SATELLITE

APPLE, launched in 1981, served as an experimental communication satellite and paved the way for the INSAT series.

Technical Details:

  • Launch Date: June 19, 1981
  • Mass: 670 kg
  • Orbit: Geostationary Transfer Orbit (GTO)
  • Launch Vehicle: Ariane 1 (France)
