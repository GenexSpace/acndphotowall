GAGANYAAN – INDIA’S FIRST HUMAN SPACEFLIGHT PROGRAM (UPCOMING 2025)

Gaganyaan is India’s first crewed spaceflight program, aiming to send three astronauts to low Earth orbit for up to seven days. Developed by ISRO with assistance from international partners, it includes an uncrewed test mission and will launch on the GSLV Mk III rocket. Planned Orbital Details:
- Orbit: ~400 km circular Low Earth Orbit
- Inclination: ~41°
Technical Specifications:
- Crew Module: Pressurized, 3 astronauts
- Launch Vehicle: GSLV Mk III
- Life Support: Environmental control and life support system (ECLSS)
- Abort System: Integrated Crew Escape System
