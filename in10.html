ADITYA-L1 – INDIA’S FIRST SOLAR MISSION (2023)

Aditya-L1 is India’s first dedicated mission to study the Sun. Launched in 2023, the spacecraft is stationed at Lagrange Point 1, allowing continuous observation of the Sun. It studies solar activity, such as solar flares and coronal mass ejections, which affect space weather and satellite operations on Earth. The data aids in understanding solar dynamics and their impact on Earth’s magnetosphere.
