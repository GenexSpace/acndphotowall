India’s Thriving Private Space Sector

India's private space ecosystem has grown rapidly since reforms were introduced in 2020. The sector includes startups and companies developing launch vehicles, satellite platforms, propulsion systems, and space services.

Agencies like IN-SPACe have been established to support and regulate private entities. Pioneers like Skyroot Aerospace and Pixxel have already launched missions, contributing to India’s growing commercial space economy.

Specifications:

Year of Reform:2020
Support Agencies:IN-SPACe, ISRO
Focus:Launch vehicles, satellites, services
