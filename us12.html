DRAGON & CREW DRAGON – COMMERCIAL SPACE TRAVEL (2012–PRESENT)

Developed by SpaceX, Dragon is the first private spacecraft to carry cargo to the ISS. Its crewed version, Crew Dragon, became the first commercial vehicle to carry astronauts to orbit (Demo-2, May 2020). These spacecraft are reusable and launched atop Falcon 9 rockets. They represent a new era of commercial partnerships in space exploration. Crew Dragon is part of NASA’s Commercial Crew Program, which ensures the U.S. maintains independent access to space.
