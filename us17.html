Man on the Moon – Apollo 12 (1969)

Apollo 12 was the second crewed lunar landing mission, launched on November 14, 1969. Commanded by Charles 'Pete' Conrad with Alan L. Bean and Richard F. Gordon Jr., the crew successfully landed in the Ocean of Storms.

The mission proved the ability to achieve pinpoint landings and retrieved parts of Surveyor 3 for analysis. Apollo 12 expanded lunar science and demonstrated improvements in EVA and lunar surface operations.

Specifications:

Launch:Nov 14, 1969
Landing Site:Ocean of Storms
