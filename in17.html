PSLV – India’s Reliable Workhorse

The Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) is India’s most successful and reliable launch vehicle. It has launched numerous satellites for Earth observation, navigation, and space science since its first successful flight in 1994.

Its ability to place multiple satellites in different orbits in a single launch has earned it global acclaim and made India a leader in cost-effective space launches.

Specifications:

First Flight:1993 (Maiden)
Success Rate:95%+
Payload:Sun-synchronous & Low Earth Orbits
