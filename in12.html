ADITYA-L1 – SOLAR MISSION TO STUDY THE SUN

Aditya-L1 is India’s first solar observatory, positioned at the Lagrange Point 1 to study solar weather and flares.

Mission Specs:

  • Launch: September 2, 2023
  • Orbit: Halo orbit around L1
  • Payloads: 7 instruments
