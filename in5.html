Gaganyaan – India’s First Human Spaceflight Program (2025)

Gaganyaan is India’s first crewed spaceflight mission, currently under development by ISRO. It aims to send a three-member crew to low Earth orbit (LEO) for up to seven days and return them safely to Earth. The first uncrewed flight test is expected in 2024, with the crewed mission following in 2025.

The mission involves developing a Human-Rated Launch Vehicle (HRLV), life support systems, astronaut training facilities, and crew escape mechanisms. Gaganyaan will mark India’s entry into human spaceflight and expand its strategic capabilities in space.

Specifications:

Planned Crew Launch:2025
Orbit:Low Earth Orbit (400 km)
Duration:Up to 7 days
