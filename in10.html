Aditya-L1 – India’s First Solar Mission (2023)

Aditya-L1 is India’s first solar observatory mission, launched in 2023 to study solar corona, solar wind, and solar magnetic storms. It orbits around the Lagrange Point 1 (L1), approximately 1.5 million kilometers from Earth.

With seven payloads onboard, Aditya-L1 will help improve space weather forecasting and enhance our understanding of solar dynamics that affect satellite operations and communication systems on Earth.

Specifications:

Launch Date:September 2, 2023
Orbit:L1 (1.5 million km)
Payloads:7 instruments
