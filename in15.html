SSLV – INDIA’S SMALL SATELLITE LAUNCH VEHICLE (2022)

The Small Satellite Launch Vehicle (SSLV) was launched in 2022 as a cost-effective and agile solution for small satellite missions.

SSLV is designed for quick integration and launch on demand, helping startups and academic institutions to reach orbit affordably.

Key Features:


- Payload to LEO: ~500 kg


- Launch Time: Short integration cycle


- Focus: Commercial smallsat missions


- Manufacturer: ISRO


Technical Specifications:

  • Stages: 3 (All Solid)
  • Height: ~34 m
  • Payload: Up to 500 kg to 500 km LEO
  • Liftoff Mass: ~120,000 kg
  • Turnaround Time: ~1 week
