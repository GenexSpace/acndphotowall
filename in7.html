RLV-TD – INDIA’S SPACE SHUTTLE PROTOTYPE (2016)

The Reusable Launch Vehicle – Technology Demonstrator (RLV-TD) was India’s first step toward developing a reusable space shuttle. Launched in 2016, it demonstrated hypersonic flight, autonomous landing, and re-entry technology. The winged body prototype resembled a small-scale spaceplane and was part of ISRO's long-term vision of reducing launch costs and enabling frequent access to space.
