BHASKARA – EARTH OBSERVATION SATELLITE SERIES

The Bhaskara satellites focused on Earth observation and remote sensing for land, water and agriculture monitoring.

Technical Details:

  • Instruments: TV camera, Microwave Radiometer
  • Orbit: Sun-synchronous
  • Launch Dates: Bhaskara-I (1979), Bhaskara-II (1981)
