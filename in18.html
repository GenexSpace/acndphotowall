DR. VIKRAM SARABHAI – FATHER OF THE INDIAN SPACE PROGRAM

Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, often called the father of India’s space program, established ISRO in 1969 and envisioned the use of space for national development.

He championed space science and education, leading India’s early satellite and rocket programs.

Contributions:


- Founded ISRO in 1969


- Championed the first sounding rockets and satellite programs


- Instrumental in setting up institutions like PRL and IIM-A


Technical Overview:

  • Legacy: Founder of Physical Research Laboratory (PRL)
  • Focus: Applications of space for communication, education
  • ISRO Foundation Year: 1969
