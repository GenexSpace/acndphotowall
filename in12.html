A SATELLITE ON A BULLOCK CART

In 1981, India transported its APPLE communication satellite on a bullock cart as part of antenna testing. The unusual method was used to avoid magnetic interference from vehicles. The image became iconic, symbolizing India’s humble yet determined space journey. APPLE was India’s first experimental geostationary satellite and laid the foundation for INSAT.
