MANGALYAAN – MARS ORBITER MISSION (2013)

Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission) made India the first Asian country to reach Mars orbit and the first to do so in its maiden attempt.

Mission Specs:

  • Launch Date: November 5, 2013
  • Orbit: Mars
  • Payloads: 5 scientific instruments
  • Cost: $74 million
