Vikram Sarabhai – Father of Indian Space Program

Dr. Vikram Sarabhai is considered the architect of India’s space program. He envisioned the peaceful use of space technology for nation-building in the 1960s.

His legacy lives on through institutions like ISRO, PRL, and IIM Ahmedabad, and the Vikram lander on India's Moon missions is named in his honor.

Specifications:

Born:August 12, 1919
Contribution:Founder of ISRO, PRL
Legacy:Namesake of Vikram lander
