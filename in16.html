Rakesh Sharma – First Indian in Space (1984)

Wing Commander Rakesh Sharma was the first Indian citizen to travel to space aboard the Soviet Soyuz T-11 mission in April 1984.

He spent nearly 8 days aboard the Salyut 7 space station and famously responded, 'Sare Jahan Se Accha' when asked how India looked from space.

Specifications:

Launch Date:April 2, 1984
Mission:Soyuz T-11
Duration:7 days, 21 hours
