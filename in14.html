Nike-Apache – India’s First Sounding Rocket

The Nike-Apache was the first sounding rocket launched from India on November 21, 1963 from Thumba Equatorial Rocket Launching Station (TERLS). The mission marked the beginning of India’s journey into space research and sounding rocket programs.

It demonstrated India's capability to support atmospheric and ionospheric experiments and laid the foundation for the country's future satellite and launch vehicle programs under ISRO.

Specifications:

Launch Date:November 21, 1963
Location:Thumba, Kerala
Type:Sounding Rocket
