NAVIC – INDIA’S REGIONAL NAVIGATION SYSTEM

NAVIC (Navigation with Indian Constellation) is a regional satellite navigation system for India and surrounding regions.

Constellation Info:

  • Satellites: 7 (3 GEO + 4 IGSO)
  • Covers: India + 1500 km region
  • Use: Navigation, timing, defense
