WAC Corporal – First US Sounding Rocket

The WAC Corporal was the first U.S. sounding rocket to reach the upper atmosphere. Developed by the Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) and the U.S. Army, it was first launched in 1945 to study atmospheric conditions and test rocket technologies.

It reached altitudes of about 70 kilometers and helped lay the groundwork for future military and scientific rockets. It represents one of the earliest steps in America’s ascent into space.

Year:1945
