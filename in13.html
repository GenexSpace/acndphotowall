INDIA’S THRIVING PRIVATE SPACE SECTOR

India’s private space industry is booming thanks to liberalization policies, ISRO’s support, and new space startups. Companies like Skyroot Aerospace, AgniKul Cosmos, and Pixxel are developing launch vehicles, satellite platforms, and Earth observation services. The IN-SPACe agency helps facilitate public-private partnerships, opening the door for innovation and commercial space opportunities in India.
