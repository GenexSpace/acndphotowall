SPACEX – REUSABLE ROCKETS (2008–PRESENT)

Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, SpaceX revolutionized the aerospace industry with its reusable rocket technology. In 2015, it became the first private company to land a rocket booster (Falcon 9) vertically after launch. This innovation drastically reduced launch costs, enabling more frequent and affordable access to space. SpaceX now regularly sends cargo and astronauts to the ISS, and its Starlink constellation provides internet globally. The company’s vision includes interplanetary travel through Starship, aiming to make humans a multiplanetary species.
