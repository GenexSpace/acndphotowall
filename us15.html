STARSHIP – THE FUTURE OF SPACE TRAVEL (2020-PRESENT)

Starship is SpaceX’s ambitious next-generation spacecraft, designed to be fully reusable. This revolutionary spacecraft aims to significantly lower the cost of space travel and make missions to Mars, the Moon, and beyond practical and affordable.

Starship is composed of two main parts: the Super Heavy booster and the Starship spacecraft itself. Both stages are designed to land vertically and be reused multiple times, dramatically reducing the cost per launch. With its stainless steel structure, the spacecraft can endure high temperatures during reentry, facilitating repeated use.

The ultimate goal of the Starship program is to establish a permanent human presence on Mars and to open the door to deep space exploration, enabling humanity to become multiplanetary.

Specifications:

Total Height:120 m (394 ft)
Diameter:9 m (30 ft)
Payload to LEO:100-150 metric tons
Material:Stainless Steel
Reusability:Fully Reusable
Planned Missions:Mars colonization, Lunar missions, Earth-orbit satellites, deep-space exploration
